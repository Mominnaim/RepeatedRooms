Backround story

The father takes her daughter out for a late night walk, during the walk there is heavy wind and a faint whisper. Later some sort of creature snatches the girl and runs. The father sets off in the woods to find the monster, and get her daughter

THE BASICS OF THE GAME

The player will have to repeatedly pick one of the three paths given, and each of those three paths will have one of the following things mentioned below:

  • Monster -> If you click this path you lose the game.
  • Item -> If you click this path, you obtain an item.
  • Safe Path -> If you click this path you either receive a battery or flashlight.

ITEM USAGE INFO

There are items that you can collect in the game, ans use it to get your through the game.

Flashlight

  • Collect batteries
    • You already have a flashlight so there will be no need to make one, just collect batteries.
    • Reveals where the monster is.

Fire Lamp

  • You need matches
    • You have a fire lamp, so no need to collect one
    • Reveals the path that has an item.

Deagle

  • You need pistol and magazine
    • You can now hunt down the monster, but be wise since you have to guess what path the monster is on.
      • If you miss, you have wasted a bullet, and need to collect another one to try again.
